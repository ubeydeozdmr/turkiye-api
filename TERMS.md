Last updated: 2026-06-13

## Free Public API

TurkiyeAPI is free to use. The service is provided to make Türkiye administrative-division data easier to access for developers, researchers, and public-interest projects.

There is no paid service-level agreement, uptime guarantee, or support guarantee.

You may use the API for lawful applications, research, internal tools, websites, and other products that need administrative-division data.

- Use the API for fraud, phishing, deceptive services, illegal activity, or harmful automation.

- Attempt to bypass rate limits, blocks, or abuse-prevention controls.

- Probe, scan, or test the production service in a way that creates operational risk.

## Rate Limits and Blocking

Rate limits may be applied to protect the service. Excessive, harmful, or abusive traffic may be throttled, blocked, or otherwise restricted.

The default public limits are documented in [README.md](./README.md#rate-limiting), but limits may change when needed to protect reliability.

## Data Accuracy

TurkiyeAPI combines public administrative data sources and includes official, derived, and estimated postal-code statuses where applicable.

The data is provided for convenience and should be independently verified before use in critical systems, legal workflows, emergency services, financial decisions, or other high-impact contexts.

## Changes

Endpoints, response fields, datasets, limits, and infrastructure may change over time. The project aims to avoid breaking changes in the stable v2 API where practical, but no compatibility guarantee is provided.
