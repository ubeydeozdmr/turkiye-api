# Code of Conduct

## Purpose

TurkiyeAPI is an open source project for developers who need reliable Türkiye administrative-division data. This code of conduct exists to keep project discussions direct, useful, and respectful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful of different backgrounds, experience levels, and viewpoints.
- Keep feedback specific, technical, and actionable.
- Assume good intent, but take responsibility for the impact of your words and actions.
- Stay on topic in issues, pull requests, and discussions.
- Respect maintainers' time by providing clear reproduction steps, context, and proposed changes when possible.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Spam, trolling, repeated off-topic comments, or deliberate disruption.
- Abusive demands for support, timelines, or unpaid work.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, block users, or take other reasonable action when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

If you need to report a conduct issue, email `ubeydeozdmr@gmail.com`. Include relevant links, screenshots, and context when possible. Reports will be handled with care and shared only with people who need the information to respond.
